# Vehicle art attribution

`materials/quantum-express-ribbon-source.png` was generated for this project with ChatGPT's built-in image generator on 2026-07-15. The complete prompt is recorded in `livery-prompt.md`.

The minibus mesh, UV layout, materials, exact text, registration plates, safety label, preview lighting, and exported game asset are original project work produced by the reproducible scripts in `tools/vehicle/`. The generated ribbon was palette-quantized and composed into the final texture by `build-texture.py`.

The supplied side-profile Toyota Quantum photograph was used only as private visual reference for general high-roof proportions, window rhythm, wheel placement, cab-over nose, sliding-door layout, bumpers, and livery placement. The photograph is not redistributed here. No Toyota badge, manufacturer logo, stock-photo watermark, or commercial operator mark was copied. The in-game vehicle remains the fictional `Quantum Express`.
