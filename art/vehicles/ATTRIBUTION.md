The minibus mesh, UV layout, materials, exact text, registration plates, safety label, preview lighting, and exported game asset are original project work produced by the reproducible scripts in `tools/vehicle/`. The Citi Golf, Vrrr Phaa GTI, Hilux Bakkie, and JMPD Interceptor meshes, opaque PBR materials, fictional plates, wheel assemblies, liveries, and preview lighting are original project work generated from `road-cars.json`. They contain no downloaded mesh, manufacturer badge, logo, or third-party texture. Their familiar in-game names predate these assets; the geometry uses broad vehicle-class cues rather than copied production bodywork.

The supplied side-profile Toyota Quantum photograph was used only as private visual reference for general high-roof proportions, window rhythm, wheel placement, cab-over nose, sliding-door layout, bumpers, and livery placement. The photograph is not redistributed here. No Toyota badge, manufacturer logo, stock-photo watermark, or commercial operator mark was copied. The in-game vehicle remains the fictional `Quantum Express`.
